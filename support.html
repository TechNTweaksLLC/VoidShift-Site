VoidShift Troubleshooting

  • Make sure your device is running the latest available iOS version.
  • Close and reopen VoidShift if the game appears frozen or progress does not update correctly.
  • Use Restore Purchases in the app settings if a previously purchased item does not appear.
  • Sign in to Game Center in iOS Settings if leaderboards are unavailable.
