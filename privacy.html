VoidShift Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 11, 2026

VoidShift is developed and published by Tech N Tweaks LLC. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected or processed when you use VoidShift.

Information We Do Not Collect

VoidShift does not require a VoidShift account. We do not collect your name, email address, contacts, precise location, photos, messages, or payment card details.

Gameplay Progress Stored On Your Device

Gameplay progress, settings, unlocks, selected cosmetics, leaderboard history stored by the app, and Galactic Credits are saved locally on your device.

Game Center

VoidShift may use Apple Game Center for leaderboards and score comparison. Game Center is provided by Apple. VoidShift does not receive or store your Game Center friends list.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. VoidShift does not receive or store payment card information. The app may store purchase entitlement status and Galactic Credit balances on your device.

Analytics And Diagnostics

VoidShift may use Firebase Analytics to process anonymous gameplay, store interaction, and diagnostic events. These events help improve balance, stability, and the player experience.

Examples may include runs started, waves completed, score, selected ship, game mode, power-ups collected, boss progress, store opens, purchase flow results, and similar gameplay or app interaction events.

Tracking

VoidShift does not use analytics data to track you across other companies' apps or websites.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple Game Center, for leaderboards and score features.
  • Apple StoreKit and the App Store, for in-app purchases.
  • Firebase Analytics, for anonymous usage and diagnostic events.

Children's Privacy

VoidShift is not intended to collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us so we can review and respond.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Tech N Tweaks LLC at support@techntweaks.com.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date on this page.
